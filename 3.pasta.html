how to make rose pasta

Ingredients

  • 300 g pasta of choice, I used penne
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small red onion, minced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • pinch sugar
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/3-1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta to al dente according to package instructions, then drain, reserving some of the pasta water.
  2. Add the tomato passata, dried basil, crumbled chicken bouillon cube, sugar, red chili flakes and salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for a few minutes, then add the cream, milk and the shredded mozzarella.
  3. Add the cooked pasta, toss to combine and warm through for a few minutes. If sauce is too thick, add a splash of pasta water. Enjoy immediately!
